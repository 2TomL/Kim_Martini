About

I am Kim Martini. A girl in love with music and vinyl. Acid music fuels my soul. Spinning records, chasing energy, dancing till sunrise. What began as beatmatching basics before and during the pandemic has grown into a daily ritual. During lockdown, music became more than an occupation, it became a passion— it also became survival. Now, I can’t imagine a day without spinning vinyl. Acid music sets me free. It drives my mind, body, and soul.
