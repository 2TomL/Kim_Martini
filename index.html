About

I’m Kim, just a girl in love with vinyl. Acid music fuels my soul. Spinning records chasing energy, and dancing ‘til sunrise. I’m a vinyl-only DJ, and my deep love for music has been nothing short of transformative. What started with learning the basics of beatmatching just before the pandemic soon grew into a daily ritual of self-expression through sound. During lockdown, music became more than just a passion for me — it became essential. Now I can’t imagine a day or night spinning vinyl records (@ home); and I’m finally hearing the difference. Acid music will set you free, it drives my mind, soul and body.
